"Bahubali" is an epic saga of royalty, betrayal, and redemption set in the mythical kingdom of Mahishmati. 
The story follows Shivudu, the son of noble warrior Amarendra Bahubali, as he discovers his true heritage and confronts the tyrannical rule of his cousin Bhallaladeva. 
Through a series of breathtaking battles and emotional revelations, Shivudu fulfills his destiny as Mahendra Bahubali, restoring justice to the kingdom and reclaiming his rightful place as its ruler, in a sweeping tale of courage and sacrifice.
